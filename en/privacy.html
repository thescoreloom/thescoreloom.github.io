Privacy Policy

TheScoreLoom™ values your privacy. Any personal data you provide through our contact form, such as your name, email, and message, is used solely to send your inquiry to us. The form does not store your data on our servers.

We never sell, share, or distribute your personal information to third parties. All data is handled securely and only used to respond to your request. Once your inquiry is processed, your information is deleted unless retention is legally required.

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we process. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@thescoreloom.it.

This Privacy Policy applies to all services and contact forms provided by TheScoreLoom™, ensuring compliance with data protection regulations and safeguarding your personal information.
