Capitolo Attivo

Ready to Play

“Ready to Play” pieces are designed for flexible ensembles, playable at various difficulty levels, and structured to avoid missing parts if some sections are absent.

You can find them on:

Sheet Music Plus

Sheet Music Direct

Noteflight

For any minor guidance or clarifications, please fill out the form on the Contact page.

Tailored Scores

If the piece you are looking for is not available in the Ready to Play section, or if you want an arrangement developed for a specific ensemble, you can request it in the Tailored Scores section. You can estimate a quote on the Estimate page, and if it meets your needs, request the arrangement by submitting the quote.

Arrangements are delivered through authorized platforms, respecting all licensing requirements.

Bespoke Scores

Direct collaboration with the composer for fully personalized works or projects. Works remain exclusive to the client (moral rights retained by the composer) for an agreed period. Can include arrangements or original compositions. Costs vary depending on complexity and rights; original compositions are priced according to the effort involved.

Post-purchase support

Tailored Scores include three months of minor internal adjustments and notation checks to optimize the performance for your ensemble.

Bespoke Scores include six months of minor internal adjustments and performance guidance to assist your ensemble.
