Cookie Policy

This page explains the use of cookies on TheScoreLoom, their purpose, and how you can manage them.

Types of Cookies Used

This site uses only:

  • Technical cookies: necessary for the website to function properly (e.g., to remember the cookie banner preference).
  • Third-party cookies: used by external services such as YouTube to display video previews.

No personal data is collected through forms or other tools.

Managing Cookies

You can accept or reject cookies via the banner on the site or by adjusting your browser settings. Rejecting cookies may limit some features, such as YouTube video previews.

Third-Party Cookies – YouTube

To display YouTube video previews in the sidebar, your consent is required. Embedded videos may use cookies that collect information such as interactions with the content (views, watch time). These cookies are managed directly by YouTube and are subject to Google’s Privacy Policy.

Changes to this Policy

This cookie policy may be updated to comply with new regulations or website changes. Please check this page periodically.
