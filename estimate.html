Pricing Breakdown

Tailored arrangements involve two components:

Professional Consultation Fee:

a fixed fee covering analysis of your ensemble, assessment of strengths and limitations, development of the arrangement plan and three months of minor internal adjustments and performance guidance to optimize execution for your ensemble.

Licensed Score Cost:

a separate payment only for the scores under copyright, processed through Hal Leonard or other authorized channels. The ensemble receives the arrangement only via official platforms.
